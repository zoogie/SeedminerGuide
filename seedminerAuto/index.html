3DS Guide for Seedminer


Getting your movable.sed

The process of getting your movable.sed is split in two parts. First you need the movable_part1.sed and then brute-force the actual movable.sed from it. You can either brute-force it yourself or ask someone to do this for you. First try the automated way, but if it does not work or shows a red error message at the top of the linked website, follow the steps of the manual way.

The automated way

Choose one of the following option:

  • I do have a somewhat powerful graphics card

    Open this website and input your Friend Code and your ID0. Click "Go" and then add the displayed Friend Code on your system. Wait until it finishes and click download movable_part1.sed. After downloading the file you can close the website.

    Once you have your movable_part1.sed continue with Section II - Checking Python

  • I do not have a powerful graphics card

    Open this website and input your Friend Code and your ID0. Click "Go" and then add the displayed Friend Code on your system. Wait until it finishes retrieving your movable_part1.sed.
    When finished click "Continue" to let one of the online miners brute-force your movable.sed. This step can take up to 30min. If it did not finish after this amount of time, click "Cancel" and use the manual way below.
    As soon as the brute-forcing finishes, you can download your movable.sed and close the website.

    Once you have your movable.sed continue with the DSiWare Injection Service

The manual way

Choose one of the following option:

  • I do have a somewhat powerful graphics card

    Join our discord server and ask in our 3ds-assistance channels if someone has the time to help you get the movable_part1.sed. Tell them you are going to brute-force yourself. You might receive a file named XX_XXXX-XXXX-XXXX_part1.sed, just rename it to movable_part1.sed. Please have your Friend Code ready when asking for help!

    Once you have your movable_part1.sed continue with Section II - Checking Python

  • I do not have a powerful graphics card

    Join our discord server and ask in our 3ds-assistance channels if someone has the time to help you bruteforce your movable.sed. Please have your Friend Code and ID0 ready when asking for help!

    Once you have your movable.sed continue with the DSiWare Injection Service
